Exhibitors

The Clio Cloud Conference brings together 600 forward-thinking legal professionals eager to learn about new technologies and services at a thought-provoking conference that’s second to none.

View last year's exhibitors

We’re proud to offer exhibitor packages that are all-inclusive, beneficial, and unique. Our exhibitor areas are carefully positioned in high-traffic locations across the venue to ensure you reach as many people as possible—creating maximum impact with minimum effort! We also plan multiple conference session breaks to create even more opportunities for you to naturally chat and network with attendees.

Get unlimited access to market your product or service to hundreds of tech-savvy lawyers as well as an all-access pass to the conference floor and social events.

To create a stress-free experience, Clio takes care of (almost) all the details - we just require that all exhibitors use the provided exhibit displays for added ease.
